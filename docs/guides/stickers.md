# Stickers

Send stickers, browse your installed packs, and search for new ones.

## Send a Sticker

> **You:** Send a thumbs up sticker to @alice

The AI will search your installed stickers and send the best match.

## Browse Your Stickers

> **You:** What sticker packs do I have installed?

Uses `telegram-get-installed-stickers` to list all your packs.

> **You:** Show me my recent stickers

Uses `telegram-get-recent-stickers` to show your recently used stickers.

## Browse a Sticker Pack

> **You:** Show me the stickers in the "Pepe" pack

Uses `telegram-get-sticker-set` to list all stickers in a specific pack.

## Search for Sticker Packs

> **You:** Find sticker packs with cats

Uses `telegram-search-sticker-sets` to search Telegram's sticker catalog.

## Available Sticker Tools

| Tool | What It Does |
|------|-------------|
| `telegram-send-sticker` | Send a sticker to a chat |
| `telegram-get-installed-stickers` | List your installed sticker packs |
| `telegram-get-recent-stickers` | Show recently used stickers |
| `telegram-get-sticker-set` | Browse stickers in a pack |
| `telegram-search-sticker-sets` | Search for sticker packs |
